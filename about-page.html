About Ararat Design

Ararat Design Ltd., incorporated in 2013, is a proudly Nigerian furniture company located in Mushin, Lagos. From our inception, we have remained dedicated to delivering high-quality furniture solutions that blend durability, functionality, and modern design.

Equipped with modern machinery and advanced production techniques, we create furniture tailored to the unique needs of homes, offices, and commercial spaces. With us, space is not a problem, as we build each piece to suit the space available and enhance the beauty and efficiency of your environment.

Our team consists of skilled and competent workers whose craftsmanship and attention to detail set us apart. We redefine the way you look at furniture by bringing your imagination to reality, ensuring that every piece reflects your taste and vision.

At Ararat Design Ltd., we are driven by innovation, precision, and customer satisfaction. We continue to grow by providing dependable products and exceptional service that our clients can trust.
